

# Hello, I’m Beka  ![](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/18350557/176309783-0785949b-9127-417c-8b55-ab5a4333674e.gif)

A **Full-Stack Developer** based in **Georgia**. I leverage **2+ years of working experience** to Architect and deliver scalable, multilingual digital platforms. My passion lies in crafting high-performance, maintainable solutions that simplify complex challenges and enhance real-world operations.

Throughout my experience, I've specialized in digitizing complex workflows. This includes developing a large-scale university management platform that centralizes operations for a vast academic community. Concurrently, I've contributed to the development of diverse e-commerce platforms serving both business and individual consumers, implementing key features from secure user account handling with mobile-based authentication to sophisticated data control via administrative tools.

I’m always working to improve, with a long-term goal of becoming more **technology-agnostic** and open to diverse development approaches. I regularly explore new tools, contribute to side projects, and study software architecture.
